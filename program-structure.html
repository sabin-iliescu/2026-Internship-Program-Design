Program Structure (Modular)

  1. Home
  2. Program Structure
3.1 Core (all interns)

AI Literacy & Deep Understanding (baseline):

Foundations (LLMs, prompting, evaluation, RAG basics, responsible AI).

Productivity toolchain (code co-pilots, automation).

Assessment: scenario-based labs + capstone demo.

Business Acumen:

Industry primers (per BU vertical), delivery models, SDLC at scale, estimation & risk, stakeholder comms.

Client context briefings and case walk-throughs.

3.2 Specialization Tracks (configure per BU)

Examples: Big Data Engineering, Cloud & DevOps, Web/Mobile, QA/Automation, Data Science/ML.

Track depth tailored to BU/client needs (tooling, architecture patterns, compliance/security).

3.3 Client Involvement

Client-sourced problem statements for capstones/POCs.

Optional client juries for demos.

Alignment on IP/data boundaries and lightweight NDAs where needed.

3.4 Format & Delivery

Flexible format: Physical, online, or hybrid by location and client proximity.

Cohort length: 8–12 weeks recommended; adjustable by BU.

Cadence: daily standups, weekly checkpoints, mid-term and final demo.

3.5 University & Community Engagement

Student Talks & roadshows (Autumn/Spring) for pipeline and brand.

Leverage Tech Community events for awareness and intern participation.

Interns as event reporters/mentors to amplify reach.

Key Decision for Governance

Endorse the modular catalog (core + tracks + client options) and format flexibility.
