Program Structure (Modular Design)

Guiding Concept

A modular internship model combining a common foundation with BU-configured specialization tracks, ensuring flexibility, scalability and project relevance.

1. Core Curriculum (All Interns)
AI Literacy & Application

Foundations: LLMs, prompting, RAG, evaluation and responsible AI.

Productivity: Code copilots, automation tools and workflow accelerators.

Assessment: Scenario-based labs and a capstone demo.

Business acumen

About Nagarro

Industry primers aligned with BU verticals.

Delivery models, SDLC at scale, estimation, risk and stakeholder communication.

Client context briefings and case walk-throughs.

2. Specialization tracks (Configured per BU)

Examples: Big Data Engineering, Cloud & DevOps, Web/Mobile, QA Automation, Data Science/ML.

Tailored by BU/client needs: covering tools, architecture, security and compliance.

3. Project involvement

Project-sourced problem statements for capstones and POCs.

Project juries for final demos and feedback.

Pre-cleared IP/data templates and lightweight NDAs for compliant collaboration.

4. Format & Delivery

Format: Physical, online, or hybrid depending on location and project team proximity.

Duration: 8–12 weeks (adjustable by BU).

Cadence: Daily stand-ups, weekly checkpoints, mid-term review and final demo.

5. University & Tech Community Engagement

Seasonal student talks (Tech Maps) and roadshows (Community Days - Autumn/Spring) to fuel pipeline and brand visibility.

Collaboration with tech communities for awareness and event participation.

Interns act as event reporters or peer mentors to amplify reach.

6. Governance & Operating Model
Roles & Responsibilities

Sponsor: Regional Governance team – executive accountability and funding.

Program Owners (PMOs): End-to-end planning, risk, budget and reporting: Sabin Iliescu and (EB representative)

BU Representatives: Demand forecasting/tentative demands open through TAP portal, track configuration, mentor nomination, allocation of graduates.

Mentors: Ensure quality standards and technical rigor.

University relations & Brand: University events, Social media campaigns, CSR tie-ins.

TA / HR Ops / IT / HRBP / EB: Hiring, Onboarding, devices, access, employee ambassadorship initiatives and policy adherence.
