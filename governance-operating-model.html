Roles & Responsibilities

Sponsor: Regional Leadership (exec accountability, funding).

Program Owner (PMO): Senior PM accountable for end-to-end plan, risk, budget, reporting.

BU Leads: demand intake, track configuration, mentor nomination, placement of converts.

SMEs: maybe check for quality bar.

University Relations & Brand: roadshows, content, campaigns, CSR tie-ins.

Compliance/IT/HR Ops: onboarding, devices, access, policy adherence.

RACI (high-level)

Approve: Sponsor, Regional Governance.

Accountable: Program Owner (PMO).

Consulted: BU Leads, Client Partners, Faculty, HR/IT/Finance.

Informed: University partners, Community leads, Internal SMEs that want to participate.
