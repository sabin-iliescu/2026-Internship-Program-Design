Context (Why now)

BUs face ongoing capability needs; current internship models are tech-siloed and inconsistent across regions.

We propose a modular "Custom Internship" anchored in BU demand, with an AI literacy baseline for all interns and configurable tracks per BU/client.

Proposal (What we'll run)

Core (all interns): AI literacy & productivity (LLMs, prompting, evaluation, RAG basics, responsible AI) + business acumen (vertical primers, delivery models, stakeholder comms).

Tracks (by BU): Web/Mobile, Backend, Big Data Eng, QA/Automation, Cloud/DevOps, Data Science/ML.

Client involvement: scoped capstones/POCs using sandboxed data, optional client juries.

Format: 8–12 weeks, physical/online/hybrid as needed; standups, weekly checkpoints, mid-term & final demo.

Operating Model & Roles

Sponsor: Regional Exec; Governance: decision gate approvals; PMO: end-to-end delivery; BU Leads: demand, mentors, placement; HR/IT/Finance/Brand/Compliance: hiring ops, tooling, budget control, campaigns, IP/data safety.

RACI: One master lifecycle RACI + 4 mini-RACIs (Funding & Mentor %, Client POCs/IP, Placement & Conversion, Tooling/Access).

ROI & KPIs

Primary: FTE conversion 55–70% within 3 months; time-to-billability ≤ 6–8 weeks; 12-month retention ≥ 85%.

Secondary: employer brand reach (impressions, events, MoUs), client-accepted POCs, innovation outputs.

Reporting cadence: weekly dashboard during cohorts; ROI reviews at 3/6/12 months.

Budget & Funding (Lens)

Direct: stipends, training/labs/certs, licenses (copilots, cloud), devices.

Indirect: mentor time, onboarding/admin, facilities.

Recommendation: Central seed + BU co-pay for Wave 1; mentor time capped at 10–15% per mentor, pre-approved as enablement and tracked monthly with Finance.

Guardrails: budget variance ≤ ±10%; mentor-cap compliance ≥ 90%.

Risks & Mitigations (Top 4)

Demand mismatch: quarterly refresh, waitlist, seat rebalancing.

Low conversion: higher entry bar, stronger client context, mid-program interview pipeline.

Cost creep (mentor/licensing): monthly burn reviews, pooled licenses, enforce mentor caps.

IP/data issues: standard NDA/IP templates, sandboxed datasets, Compliance sign-off gate.

Timeline & Gates

Oct–Nov 2025: Governance alignment (this memo).

Dec 2025: BU demand lock; locations; mentor nominations; content refresh kickoff.

Jan–Feb 2026: Roadshows & applications; screening & offers.

Mar 2026: Onboarding readiness (IT/licensing/labs).

Apr–Jun 2026: Cohort Wave 1 → Mid-term gate.

Jul–Sep 2026: Optional Wave 2.

Approvals Requested (Today)

Guiding Principles — Approve the business-led, custom, AI-baseline, client-aware design.

Funding & Mentor Accounting — Select Central seed + BU co-pay; approve mentor cap 10–15% counted as enablement.

KPI Pack & Reporting Cadence — Approve targets and weekly + 3/6/12-month ROI reviews.

Milestones — Approve Dec 2025 demand lock and Apr–Jun 2026 Cohort 1 (optional Jul–Sep Cohort 2).

What approval unlocks: budget allocation & mentor caps, BU demand finalization, university campaign launch, tooling/licensing procurement, and detailed syllabus build.
