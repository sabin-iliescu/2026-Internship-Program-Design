Program Overview

Objectives (Business Demand as Baseline)

Translate BU demand forecasts into capacity

Translate BU demand forecasts into capacity (headcount, locations, timelines).

Prioritize skill families

Prioritize skill families (e.g., Frontend, Backend, Data/ML, QA, DevOps, Cloud, Big Data) informed by deal flow and attrition backfills.

Define success

Define success via conversion to FTE, client-ready capability, and brand reach.

Key Decision for Governance

Key Decision for Governance: Approve demand-driven intake model and skill mix approach.

Guiding Principles

Business-led, ROI-driven

Intake, scope, and skill focus originate from validated BU demand plans, not from technology silos.

Custom over one-size-fits-all

A modular "Custom Internship" that adapts to BU/client needs (duration, track mix, format).

AI-first foundation

Every intern reaches an AI literacy baseline; advanced tracks deepen where relevant.

Client-aware by design

Structure and deliverables reflect actual client contexts and pipelines.

Brand & community impact

Interns act as ambassadors via CSR, tech talks, and events.

Operational clarity

Clear roles, decision gates, and resourcing rules to avoid hidden costs.

ROI & Measurement

Primary KPIs

Conversion to FTE: target ≥ 55–70% within 3 to 6 months of program.

Time-to-billability for converts: ≤ 6–8 weeks to productive deployment.

Retention of converts: ≥ 85% at 12 months.

Secondary KPIs

Employer brand reach: impressions, event attendance, university MoUs.

Client-visible outcomes: POCs accepted, re-useable assets created.

Innovation signal: submissions to global contests, internal accelerators, patents/whitepapers.

Data Cadence

Weekly ops dashboard during program; 3-, 6-, 12-month post-mortems on converts.

Key Decision for Governance

Approve KPI set and reporting cadence.

Risks & Mitigations

Under-utilized mentors / cost creep

fix mentor % allocations; monthly burn review; BU co-funding.

Mismatch to BU demand

quarterly demand refresh; agile seat rebalancing; waitlist.

Low conversion

raise entry bar, increase hands-on client context, structured interview pipeline mid-program.

Brand without pipeline

align roadshows to intake windows; "Apply now" CTAs and clear timelines.

Data/IP concerns for client POCs

pre-cleared problem templates; red-team reviews; sandboxed datasets.
